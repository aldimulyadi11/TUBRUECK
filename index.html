    HISTORY OF TUBRUCK

          This company was initiated by our founder as he was working in Germany in early 2018, the initiation emerged based on the lack of information on Indonesian coffee but positive responses continued to arrive for Indonesian coffee. This opportunity created several other opportunities, such as the emergence of a sense of patriotism to the country through the desire to show the quality of Indonesian products, especially coffee, with the aim of improving the quality of life and the business of farmers in Indonesia to meet the needs of the coffee market in Europe by bringing together farmers and customers. directly through the help of Information Technology. In 2019 we participated in the WOC (World of Coffee) Berlin, Germany by collaborating with another Indonesian coffee brand to become one of the tenant participants in the activity with an unexpected response that the quality of Indonesian coffee received a positive response from various parties. Following on from the event, we have conducted sampling curation and scoring of Indonesian coffee with the assessment that Indonesian coffee is very worthy of being sold and enjoyed by the world. Based on the series of journeys that we have experienced, we are determined to be a portal for transactions and delivery of Indonesian coffee worldwide.
